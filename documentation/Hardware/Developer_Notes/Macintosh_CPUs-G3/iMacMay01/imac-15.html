Microprocessor and Cache

The microprocessor runs at a clock speed of 400, 500, or 600 MHz. The microprocessor used in the 600-MHz models and some 500-MHz models is a G3 with a built-in level 2 (L2) cache. The microprocessor in the 400-MHz model has an external L2 cache.
