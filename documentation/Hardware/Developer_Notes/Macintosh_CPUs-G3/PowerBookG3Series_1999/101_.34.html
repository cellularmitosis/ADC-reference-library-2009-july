Other Alphanumeric Keys

When the embedded numeric keypad is active, the other alphanumeric keys can be made to have no operation (NOP). The affected keys include certain special character keys: equal sign, right and left brackets, backslash, and straight apostrophe. These keys are affected by the Num Lock key.
