Power Management Unit

The power manager IC is a 68HC05 microprocessor, also called the Cuda PMU. It operates with its own RAM and ROM. The functions of the Cuda PMU include:

  • controlling the sleep and power on and off sequences
  • controlling power to the other ICs
  • controlling the brightness of the display
  • supporting the ADB interface to the built-in keyboard and trackpad
  • monitoring the battery charge level
  • controlling battery charging
