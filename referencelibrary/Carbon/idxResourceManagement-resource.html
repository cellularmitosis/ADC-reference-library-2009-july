Leopard Reference Library: Carbon
Resource Management
Applications often need to load specific resources in addition to their executable code. Carbon includes support for distributing, locating, and loading these resources, which include memory allocations, feature availability information, power and energy resources, and application-specific images, sounds, and localized strings.
