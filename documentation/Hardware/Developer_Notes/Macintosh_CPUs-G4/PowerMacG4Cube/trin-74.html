Mechanical Design of RAM DIMMs

The mechanical characteristics of the RAM expansion DIMM are given in the JEDEC specification for the 168-pin 8-byte DRAM DIMM. The specification number is JEDEC MO-161-D. To obtain the specification, see the references at RAM Expansion Modules.

The maximum height of DIMMs for use in the Power Mac G4 Cube is 1.38 inches.
