Sound Specifications

The frequency response of the sound circuits, not including the microphone and speakers, is within plus or minus 1 dB from 4 Hz to 20 kHz.

Total harmonic distortion plus noise (THD+N) as a percentage of full scale for various outputs are shown in Table 3-3 .
