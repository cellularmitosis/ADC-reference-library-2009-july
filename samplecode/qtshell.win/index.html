Description

QTShell is a simple QuickTime and QuickTime VR viewer framework. It demonstrates how to incorporate QuickTime and QuickTime VR movie support into an application. Can be compiled and run under the Mac OS and under Windows. Requirements: 68k or PowerPC (or newer) machines running System 8.5 or later. PC with Intel Pentium or compatible processor. QuickTime 4.0 or later. CodeWarrior or Visual C++ 6.0 or later. Keywords: QuickTime, VR, Framework, Shell, Viewer
