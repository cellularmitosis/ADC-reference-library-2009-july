Hard Disk Dimensions

Figure 3-2 shows the maximum dimensions of the hard disk and the location of the mounting holes. The hard disk is physically smaller than the ones in previous PowerBook computers: only 12.7 mm (0.5 inches) high.

The minimum clearance between any conductive components on the drive and the bottom of the mounting envelope is 0.5 mm.
