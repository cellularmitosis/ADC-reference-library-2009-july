Digital Display Resolutions

Table 3-8 shows the resolutions supported on flat-panel (digital) displays. The 16 MB of video RAM on the accelerated graphics card supports pixel depths up to 32 bits per pixel at all resolutions.

Table 3-8  Digital display resolutions

640 by 480   1024 by 768
800 by 500   1280 by 800*
800 by 512   1280 by 1024
800 by 600   1600 by 1024
1024 by 600   1600 by 1200
*available only on the ATI card
