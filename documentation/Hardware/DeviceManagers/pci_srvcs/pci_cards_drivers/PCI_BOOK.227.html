Supported DLPI Primitives

The following DLPI primitives are supported by the Open Transport CSMA/CD driver. The ones marked with a are not required by either the Appletalk or TCP/IP stacks:

DL_BIND_ACK
DL_BIND_REQ
DL_DISABLEMULTI_REQ
DL_ENABLEMULTI_REQ
DL_ERROR_ACK
DL_INFO_ACK
DL_INFO_REQ
DL_OK_ACK
DL_PHYS_ADDR_ACK
DL_PHYS_ADDR_REQ
DL_SUBS_BIND_ACK
DL_SUBS_BIND_REQ
DL_TEST_CON
DL_TEST_IND
DL_TEST_REQ
DL_TEST_RES
DL_UNBIND_REQ
DL_UNITDATA_IND
DL_UNITDATA_REQ
DL_XID_CON
DL_XID_IND
DL_XID_REQ
DL_XID_RES

Future versions of the driver will also support these additional primitives: DL_GET_STATISTICS_ACK
DL_GET_STATISTICS_REQ
DL_PROMISCOFF_REQ
DL_PROMISCON_REQ
