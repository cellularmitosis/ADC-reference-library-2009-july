Earlier PowerBook G3 Series Computers

The new PowerBook G3 Series 1999 computer is not the same as the PowerBook G3 series or the original PowerBook G3 computer. The newest model has a slimmer case. An article in Apple's Tech Info Library (TIL) discusses ways to tell these computers apart. You can read the article on the World Wide Web at:

http://til.info.apple.com/techinfo.nsf/artnum/n24604
