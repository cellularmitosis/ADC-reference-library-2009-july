CD-ROM Drive

One configuration of the eMac has an internal CD-ROM drive. The drive has a tray for loading the disc. The drive supports 32X data transfer rates using constant angular velocity (CAV) and a data buffer.

The CD-ROM drive supports the worldwide standards and specifications for CD-ROM and CD-digital audio discs described in the Sony/Philips Yellow Book and Red Book. The drive can read CD-ROM, CD-ROM XA, CD-I, PhotoCD, and Video CD discs as well as play standard audio discs.

Digital audio signals from the CD-ROM drive can be played through the sound outputs under the control of the Sound Manager.

The CD-ROM drive is an ATAPI drive and is cable-selected as device 0 (master) in an ATA Device 0/1 configuration.
