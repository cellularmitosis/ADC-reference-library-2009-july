CD-RW Drive

An internal CD-RW drive is available as an option. The drive has a tray for loading the disc. The drive is capable of writing CD-R media at 8x speed and CD-RW media at 4x speed. It can read CD-ROM media at 24x speed (CAV).

Digital audio signals from the CD-RW drive can be played through the sound outputs under the control of the Sound Manager.
