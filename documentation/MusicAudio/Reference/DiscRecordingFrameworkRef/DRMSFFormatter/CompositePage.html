DRMSFFormatter.h

Overview

NSFormatter subclass to format DRMSF objects.

Discussion

Instances of DRMSFFormatter format the textual representation of cells that contain MSF objects and convert textual representations of msf values into MSF objects. DRMSFFormatters are typically instantiated in IB.
