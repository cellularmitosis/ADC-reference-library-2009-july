Leopard Reference: Apple Applications
iSync
iSync transfers addresses and calendar information between the user's Macintosh computer, iPod, Palm OS-based device, Bluetooth-enabled cell phone, or USB cell phone. Developers can use the Sync Services framework to add syncing to their applications.
