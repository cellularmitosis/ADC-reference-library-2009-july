Peripheral Devices

In addition to the devices that are included with the computer, certain peripheral devices are available separately:

  • The Macintosh PowerBook Intelligent Lithium Ion Battery is available separately as an additional or replacement battery.
  • The Macintosh PowerBook 45W AC Adapter, which comes with the computer, is also available separately. The adapter can recharge the internal battery in four hours while the computer is running or in two hours while the computer is shut down or in sleep mode.
