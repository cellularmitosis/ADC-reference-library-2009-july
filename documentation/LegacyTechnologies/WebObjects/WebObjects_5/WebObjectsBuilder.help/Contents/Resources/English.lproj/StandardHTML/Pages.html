Pages

In the page's inspector, you can set the page's title, colors, and background.

To select the page:

  • Click the <BODY> or <DOCUMENT> tag in the path view.

To use a dynamic string as the page's title:

  • In the page Inspector, select "Title is dynamic" and enter the binding in the Title field.

To change the colors of the page's background, text, or links:

  • Click the edge of the appropriate color well.

To specify a graphic to use as the page's background

  • Click Textures.
