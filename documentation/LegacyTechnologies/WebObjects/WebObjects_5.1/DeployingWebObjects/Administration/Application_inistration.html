Application Administration

After deploying applications, you should monitor their performance to find out, among other things, if you need to add instances to an application to improve response times. "Monitoring Activity" shows you the kind of performance data you can collect about an application.

There are several steps you can take to improve your site's performance; most of them have been explained in the preceding chapters. In the section "Improving Performance" you'll find a list of recommended measures that will help optimize your site's operation.
