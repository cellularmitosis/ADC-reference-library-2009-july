AGP/PCI Service

A combination slot supports either a PCI or an AGP card through a personality slot video card. When used for PCI, it supports 66 MHz 32-bit only operation. When used for AGP, it supports a 4X AGP bus. This slot does not provide any ADC power. For further details, see "PCI Expansion Slots".
