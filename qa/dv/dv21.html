Technical Q&A DV21
PowerMac 9500 Sound Input

Q: The Mac 9500 develop note on the latest CD says that the sound in port for the 9500 has 4-conductor requirements. What is the 4-conductor pinout? Does the Plaintalk microphone work on the 9500?

A: The 9500 has stereo in (supporting left, right, power and ground) and requires a mini stereo plug that you can buy today - no new wacky pinouts. The Plaintalk microphone does work on the 9500.

[Sep 15 1995]
