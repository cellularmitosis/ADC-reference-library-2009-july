Verifying Your Installation

After you have installed WebObjects and performed the steps in Chapter 2, "Platform Specific Configuration", you should verify your installation. This is also useful after you have made other changes such as modifying your adaptors as presented in >Chapter 4, "Web Server Adaptors", The steps outlined below will help you to do this.

These steps assume you have access to a WebObjects Developer installation. The examples mentioned below are not installed with WebObjects Deployment, though they can be copied to, and tested on a WebObjects Deployment installation.
