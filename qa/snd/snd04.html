Technical Q&A SND04
SndPlayDoubleBuffer 16-bit Support

Q The documentation says that SndPlayDoubleBuffer doesn't work with 16-bit sounds. Is this true?

A This is no longer the case. Sound Manager 3.0 and later support playing 16-bit sounds with SndPlayDoubleBuffer.

SndPlayDoubleBuffer also supports the playing of compressed sounds when you use the SndDoubleBufferHeader2 structure.

[Jun 01 1996]
