Monitor Connector

The connector for the analog video monitor is a custom 14-pin connector. The pins and signals are listed in Table 3-8. An adapter is available for use with monitors with VGA 15-pin miniature D-type connectors.


Table 3-8 Signals on the monitor connector


Pin Signal name   Pin Signal name
1 GND   8 DDC_VCC_5
2 /VSYNC   9 ANALOG_BLU
3 /HSYNC   10 VGA_EIC_DAT
4 RED_RTN   11 VGA_EIC_CLK
5 ANALOG_RED   12 GND
6 GRN_RTN   13 MON_DETECT
7 ANALOG_GRN   14 BLU_RTN
