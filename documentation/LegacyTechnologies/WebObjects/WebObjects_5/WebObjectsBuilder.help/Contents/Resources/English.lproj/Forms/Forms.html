Forms

If you put form elements in your component, you must place them in a form container. If a form element is outside a form container, its contents are ignored. Note that if you place a Submit button outside a form container, pressing the button will not submit any data.

To add a form container to your component:

  • Choose Forms > WOForm, or press the Form Container button .

Note that if your form container contains more than one Submit or Image button, you must set the form container's submitMultiple attribute to true. Otherwise, the two buttons will perform the same action.
