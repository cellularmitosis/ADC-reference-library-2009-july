Video Monitor Ports

Depending on the configuration, the Power Mac G4 computer comes either an ATI or an Nvidia graphics card installed. The main features of the graphics cards are as follows:


Graphics IC Video RAM Connectors
ATI RV200 32 MB DDR ADC and VGA
Nvidia NV17 64 MB DDR ADC and VGA

Either graphics card can support two monitors at the same time.
