Digital Display Resolutions

Table 3-8 shows the resolutions supported on the digital interface. The 16 MB of video RAM on the accelerated graphics card supports pixel depths up to 32 bits per pixel at all resolutions.

Not all resolutions are available on all monitors.

Table 3-8   Digital display resolutions

640 by 480   1024 by 768
800 by 500   1280 by 800
800 by 512   1280 by 1024
800 by 600   1600 by 1024
1024 by 640   1600 by 1200
