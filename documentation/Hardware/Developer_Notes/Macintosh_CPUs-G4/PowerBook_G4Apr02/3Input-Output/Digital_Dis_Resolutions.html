Digital Display Resolutions

Table 3-14 (page 53) shows the resolutions supported on flat-panel (digital) displays. The 32 MB of video RAM on the accelerated graphics card supports pixel depths up to 32 bits per pixel at all resolutions.


Table 3-14 Picture sizes on a digital display

640 by 480 1024 by 768
800 by 500 1280 by 800
800 by 512 1280 by 1024
800 by 600 1344 by 840
960 by 600 1600 by 1024
1024 by 600 1600 by 1200
1024 by 640 1920 by 1200
