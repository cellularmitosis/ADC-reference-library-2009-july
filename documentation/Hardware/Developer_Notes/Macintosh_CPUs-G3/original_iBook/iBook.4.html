RAM Expansion Modules

The mechanical characteristics of the RAM expansion module are given in JEDEC specification number JEDEC MO-190-C. The electrical characteristics of the RAM SO-DIMM are given in section 4.5.6 of the JEDEC Standard 21-C, release 7. These specifications are available from the Electronics Industry Association's web site at

http://www.jedec.org/download/default.htm
