Technical Q&A QD43
Background Printing On, Print Monitor Dialog Missing

Q: When I print with background printing enabled, and print monitor fires up and tries to post an error, my app hangs and no dialog ever appears. What gives?

A: Make sure the bits in your SIZE resource are set correctly. This behavior appears when you've got your "Multifinder aware" bit set, but not your "accepts Suspend/Resume events" bit set.

[May 14 1996]
