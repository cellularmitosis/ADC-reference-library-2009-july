Choosing the Tables to Include

  1. In the wizard panel, select MOVIE, MOVIE_ROLE, and TALENT in the Tables browser.

    The wizard creates entities only for the tables you select. Since the Movies application doesn't interact with any of the other tables (DIRECTOR, PLOT_SUMMARY, STUDIO, and TALENT_PHOTO), you don't need to include them in the model.

  2. Click Next.
