AirPort Card Support

An AirPort Card, Apple's internal wireless LAN module, is available as an option. The connector for the AirPort Card uses the CardBus interface. A separate connector is used for the cable to the antennas, which are built into the computer's enclosure. For information about the operation of the AirPort Card, see "AirPort Card".
