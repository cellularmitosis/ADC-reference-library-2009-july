PCI SIG

Q: What is the meaning of the acronym PCI SIG?

A: PCI SIG stands for Peripheral Component Interchange Special Interest Group. They can be contacted at:

PCI Special Interest Group
P.O. Box 14070
Portland, OR 97214
800-433-5177 (U.S.)
503-797-4207 (International)
503-234-6762 (FAX)
