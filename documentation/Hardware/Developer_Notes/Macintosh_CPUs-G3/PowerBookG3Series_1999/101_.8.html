Expansion Bay Modules

The expansion bay in the PowerBook computer is not the same as those in the PowerBook G3 Series and PowerBook 3400 computers. Expansion bay modules designed for earlier PowerBook computers will not fit in the PowerBook computer. For more information, see Mechanical Design of Expansion Bay Modules .
