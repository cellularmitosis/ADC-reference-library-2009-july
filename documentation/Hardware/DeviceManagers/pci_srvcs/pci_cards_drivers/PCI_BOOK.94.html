Gestalt Manager

Gestalt calls are available only to applications, not to drivers. Drivers may provide driverGestalt services via the Status selector to DoDriverIO or may alternatively present device information through the Name Registry. The Name Registry is a unifying mechanism and is the preferred means for representing system information. See Driver Gestalt for additional information about the driver Gestalt services.
