VGA Video Output

The iBook computer has an output port for connecting a VGA video monitor. The computer detects when a monitor is plugged in and configures the display appropriately.

The monitor operates in mirror mode: its display duplicates the display on the internal flat panel.

Display sizes supported are 640 by 480, 800 by 600, and 1024 by 768 pixels.
