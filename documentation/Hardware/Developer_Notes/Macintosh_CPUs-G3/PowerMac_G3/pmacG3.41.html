The Modem Slot

The Apple DVD-Video and Audio/Video Card, Apple Audio/Video Card, and Apple Audio Input/Output Card all have a separate slot for an optional fax/modem card. The modem slot is not a PCI bus-based communications slot (comm slot II) like that in the Power Macintosh 5500 and 6500, and it does not accept comm slot II cards that use any of the PCI signals.
