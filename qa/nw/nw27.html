Technical Q&A NW27
TCP Option Sizes

Q: I'm trying to implement some of the TCP options defined under Open Transport. I find that the Client Guide does not specify the option sizes for all of the various options. Where can I get information on setting these options, their sizes, and their valid settings?

A: See the XTI Specification, Appendix B, Section B.2 Options. This document discusses, in detail, the IP, TCP, and UDP level options which are available under Open Transports implementation of TCP.

[May 14 1996]
