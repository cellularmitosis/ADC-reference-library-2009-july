Locations of the Expansion Slots

Figure 4-6 shows the location of the two main RAM expansion slots and the video RAM expansion slot.

One of the main RAM expansion slots is on the bottom of the processor module and is normally occupied by the factory-installed SO-DIMM. The other main RAM expansion slot is on the top of the processor module and is available for a user-installed SO-DIMM.
