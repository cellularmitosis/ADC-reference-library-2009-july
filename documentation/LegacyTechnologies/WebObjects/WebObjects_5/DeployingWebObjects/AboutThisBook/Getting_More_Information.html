Getting More Information

To get an overview of the WebObjects platform, you should read the book WebObjects Overview. You can find general information about WebObjects at http://www.apple.com/developer/webobjects.

These are other WebObjects documents that provide helpful information:

  • Installation Guide includes system requirements, compatibility information, and installation instructions. (The Installation Guide is included in your WebObjects box.)
  • Post-Installation Instructions lists tasks that you must perform after installing WebObjects Deployment to confirm that the installation was successful and to tailor it for your specific needs.
