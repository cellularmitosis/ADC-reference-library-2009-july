Infrared Communication Link

The PowerBook G4 computer has a directed infrared (IR) communication link connected internally to a serial port on the KeyLargo IC. When the computer is placed within range of another device with an IR interface, it can send and receive serial data. Other devices with an IR interface include PDAs, some digital cell phones, and some printers.

Operating range is 1 meter, and the devices must be positioned with their IR ports pointed toward each other within about 15 degrees.

The IR link in the PowerBook G4 computer supports the Infrared Data Association (IrDA) standard at up to 4.0 Mbps. The modulation method complies with the IrDA physical layer standard. For more information, see the reference at "Infrared Interface".
