Technical Q&A QTVR10
Setting the Ratio Between Height and Width

Q Does the ratio between Height and Width of the panoSourcePict have to be set to 3,25?

A You can use whatever ratio you want. However, since the next step in the process is dicing, you need to cut your panoramic into tiles. The overall height and width of the panoramic must be divisible by the tile size or it will not dice properly.

[Jun 01 1995]
