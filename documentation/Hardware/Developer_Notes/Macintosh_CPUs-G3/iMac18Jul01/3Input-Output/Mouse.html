Mouse

The iMac comes with an Apple Pro Mouse. The mouse case is made of polycarbonate plastic like the computer.

The Apple Pro Mouse uses optical tracking in place of the traditional rolling ball. It works on almost any surface, though non-reflective, opaque surface without repetitive patterns work best.
