Manager Group: Graphics Services


The graphics services allow your application to construct and render images. Carbon supports these managers, with the exception of QuickDraw GX and the Printing Manager. Carbon supplies a complete replacement for the Printing Manager. Most of the QuickDraw GX features are supported by other Carbon technologies, such as the Apple Type Services for Unicode Imaging, the ColorSync Manager, the Collection Manager, the Carbon Printing Manager, and QuickDraw.
