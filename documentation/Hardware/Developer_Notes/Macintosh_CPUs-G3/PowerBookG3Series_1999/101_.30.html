Num Lock Key

Pressing the Num Lock key affects two sets of keys: the embedded numeric keypad and the rest of the alphanumeric keys.

  • It selects the embedded numeric keypad on the right portion of the alphanumeric keys, as shown in Table 3-6 and Figure 3-6 .
  • It makes the rest of the alphanumeric keys functionless (NOPs), as shown in Figure 3-6 .

An LED next to the Num Lock key is lighted whenever the Num Lock function is active.
