Apple Speaker Mini-jack

The Apple speaker mini-jack is a 2.5-mm stereo mini jack. It has a smaller diameter than the headphone jack so that the user cannot inadvertently plug headphones into it.

WARNING

Some types of headphones and other audio devices have a 2.5-mm plug. The user should be warned not to plug such devices into the Apple speaker mini-jack. Doing so could cause damage to the devices.

The Apple Speakers include an internal ROM that enables the computer to identify the speakers. Speakers other than the Apple Speakers should not be connected to the Apple Speaker jack.
