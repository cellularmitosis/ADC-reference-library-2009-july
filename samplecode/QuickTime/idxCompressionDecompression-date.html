Leopard Sample Code: QuickTime
Compression & Decompression
Applications can use the Image Compression Manager and QuickTime codec components to compress and decompress sounds, images, and image sequences, as well as to transcode sounds and images between compression formats. Data codecs are used with other kinds of compressed data.
