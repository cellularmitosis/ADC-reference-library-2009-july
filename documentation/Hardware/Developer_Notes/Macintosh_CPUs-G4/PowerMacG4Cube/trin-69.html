Mouse

The Power Mac G4 Cube comes with an Apple Pro Mouse. The Apple Pro Mouse is a new design that uses optical tracking in place of the traditional rolling ball. It works on almost any surface, though non-reflective, opaque surface without repetitive patterns work best.
