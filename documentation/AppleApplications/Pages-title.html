Pages
Pages offers full-featured word-processing with drag-and-drop simplicity. It includes professionally designed templates with coordinated colors, fonts, tables, and charts for over 40 types of documents. It supports popular graphics formats such as GIF, JPEG, TIFF, PDF, and PSD. It imports AppleWorks, Word, and RTF documents and exports documents as Word, HTML, and RTF. Developers can use the Pages XML file format schema to create and modify Pages documents programmatically.
