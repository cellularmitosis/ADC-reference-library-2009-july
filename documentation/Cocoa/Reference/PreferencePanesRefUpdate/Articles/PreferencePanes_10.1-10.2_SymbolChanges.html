10.2 Symbol Changes

This article lists the symbols added to PreferencePanes.framework in Mac OS X v10.2.

Classes

All of the classes with new symbols are listed alphabetically, with their new class, instance, and delegate methods described.

NSPreferencePane

Complete reference information is available in the NSPreferencePane reference.

Instance Methods

autoSaveTextFieldsIf this method returns YES, text fields are forced to give up their responder status before shouldUnselect is called on the preference pane.
