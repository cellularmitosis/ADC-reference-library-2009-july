External Video Connector

The cable from the external monitor plugs into a DB-15 video port located on the enclosure's rear panel. The pin assignments for the external video connector are the same as those for the Power Macintosh 5500 and 6500 computers.

External Video Monitors

This section includes two tables; Table 3-1 provides a list of display modes supported on the external video connector of the All-in-one enclosure, and Table 3-2 lists the display modes supported on the external video connector of the modular desktop and mini-tower enclosures. The display resolution values listed in Table 3-1 and 3-2 are supported on AppleVision monitors or DDC enabled monitors with the proper cable.

The Power Macintosh G3 All-in-one can drive several sizes of external video monitors in display mirror mode. Table 3-1 shows the display resolution, vertical refresh, horizontal refresh, and maximum pixel depths supported by the Power Macintosh G3 All-in-one display graphics subsystem.
