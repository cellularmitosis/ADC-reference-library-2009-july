Technical Q&A QD3D54
QD3D Windows High Quality Postscript Output

Q: Does the Windows version of QD3D support high quality printing to PostScript?

A: The QuickDraw 3D API's for the Windows platform are identical to the Macintosh API's. Therefore, the answer here is the same as the one above - we don't provide direct support for this kind of thing. However, you can draw to a pixmap draw context and then print via a standard Windows printing loop. With third-party products you can also achieve anti-aliasing.

[Jul 11 1997]
