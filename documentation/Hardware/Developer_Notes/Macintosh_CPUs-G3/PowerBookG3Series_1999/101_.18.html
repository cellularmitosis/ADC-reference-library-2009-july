Video Display Subsystem

The display subsystem consists of a graphics controller IC, 8 MB of SDRAM on the main logic board, and ports for external video monitors.

The graphics controller IC is an ATI Rage LT Pro. It contains 2D and 3D acceleration engines, a digital video port, front-end and back-end scalers, a CRT controller, and a PCI bus interface with bus master capability.

For information about the display, see Flat Panel Display in Devices and Ports For information about external monitors and projection devices, see External Monitors also in Chapter 3.
