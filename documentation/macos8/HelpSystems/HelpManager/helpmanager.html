You can use the Help Manager to provide your users with Balloon Help online assistance: contextual information that describes the actions, behaviors, or properties of your application’s features. If you offer an additional help facility for your users, you should use the Help Manager to add your own menu items to the Help menu so that there's one convenient and consistent place for users to look for help information.
