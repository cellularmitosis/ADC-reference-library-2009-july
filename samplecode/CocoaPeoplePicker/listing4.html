CocoaPeoplePicker
v1.0
12/8/2003

This sample demonstrates how to use the AddressBook framework's
ABPeoplePickerView for Cocoa, new in Panther.  It embeds an ABPeoplePickerView
in a window, and includes controls for view options and access to data the user
has selected.

Take a look at MainMenu.nib and MyPeoplePickerController.m for how to hook an
ABPeoplePickerView into your app and integrate with the Mac OS X Address Book.

The ABPeoplePickerView is new to Mac OS X 10.3 (Panther).  It requires 10.3 or
later, and Xcode.
