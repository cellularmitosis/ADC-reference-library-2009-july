Power Saving Modes

The PowerBook G4 computer meets the following power saving standards:

  • Energy Saver

  • Energy Star (US)

  • Blue Angel (Germany)

To meet those standards, the default configuration of the computer must draw less than 7 watts in Sleep mode and less than 5 watts when the computer is off, while plugged into the AC adapter and with the battery removed.
