Fn Key

Pressing the Fn key affects three sets of keys: the function keys F1-F12, the embedded numeric keypad, and certain modifier keys.

  • It toggles the function keys between their normal control functions and their alternate F1-F12 functions, as shown in Table 3-5 and Figure 3-5 .
  • It selects the embedded numeric keypad on the right portion of the alphanumeric keys, as shown in Table 3-6 and Figure 3-6 .
  • It changes certain control keys, including the cursor control keys, to page control keys, as shown in Table 3-7 and Figure 3-6 .
