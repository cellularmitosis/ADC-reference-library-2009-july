XYZ color space
The fundamental CIE-based color space that allows colors to be expressed as a mixture of the three tristimulus values X, Y, and Z.
x-height
The height of a lowercase x in a given font. It is the height, measured from the baseline, of the main portion of most lowercase letters (excluding ascenders and descenders)
