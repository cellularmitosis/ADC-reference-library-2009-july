Paragraphs

To create a paragraph (<P>):

  • Choose Elements > Paragraph or click the Paragraph button .

    If there is a text selection, the entire selection becomes a paragraph.

To start a new paragraph when you're in a paragraph, press Return. To enter a line break (<BR>), press Shift-Return.

To change a paragraph to another element, select the paragraph and choose Window > Inspector. You can change the paragraph to one of these tags:

  • Plain (<P>)
  • Preformatted (<PRE>)
  • Address (<ADDRESS>)
  • Block quote (<BLOCKQUOTE>)
  • Division (<DIV>)

Tip: To create one of these elements without going to the Inspector, choose one of them from the Elements menu.
