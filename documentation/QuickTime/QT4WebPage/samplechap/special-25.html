Targeting QuickTime Player

Alternatively, you can have your poster movies target QuickTime Player. A user who runs short on RAM can simply close any open movies. The HTML looks like this:

<EMBED SRC="Poster1.mov" HREF="HugeMovie1.mov"
TARGET="quicktimeplayer" HEIGHT=136 WIDTH=160> <BR>
<EMBED SRC="Poster2.mov" HREF="HugeMovie2.mov"
TARGET="quicktimeplayer" HEIGHT=136 WIDTH=160> <BR>
<EMBED SRC="Poster3.mov" HREF="HugeMovie3.mov"
TARGET="quicktimeplayer" HEIGHT=136 WIDTH=160> <BR>

To see an example of this approach, load MultiMovie4.htm (in the SpecialDelivery folder on the CD).

 

One nice thing about this approach is that the movie can be larger than the space allocated to the plug-in or even larger than the browser window. If you like, it can even play in full-screen mode. For details, see Full-Screen Movies.
