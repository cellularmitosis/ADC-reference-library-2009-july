External Monitors

The computer has a DVI-I connector for an Apple digital display, an external video monitor, or a projection device. The DVI-I connector that supports all DVI-equipped displays. A DVI-to-VGA adapter for use with analog monitors is included. With the Apple DVI to ADC Adapter, available separately, the PowerBook G4 computer can be used with any digital Apple monitor that has an ADC connector.

The computer also has an S-video connector that supplies a video signal for an NTSC or PAL video monitor or VCR. See "External Video Port".
