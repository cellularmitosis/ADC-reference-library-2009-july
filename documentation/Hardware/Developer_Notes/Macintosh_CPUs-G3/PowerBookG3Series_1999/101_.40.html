Sound Inputs

The sound system accepts inputs from five possible sources:

  • built-in microphone
  • external stereo sound input jack
  • 1-bit sound from the CardBus socket
  • sound from the expansion bay
  • sound from the communication (modem) slot

The microphone and the sound input jack have dedicated input channels on the Screamer IC; the sound input from the PC Card slot has its own input, and the other three inputs share an input on the IC. Those three inputs are switched on and off by the hardware; they can be selected one at a time for play-through or recording.
