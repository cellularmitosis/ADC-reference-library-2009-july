Contents of This Note

The information in this note is arranged in five chapters and an appendix.

  • Introduction introduces the iMac family of computers and describes their features.

  • Architecture describes the internal logic of the computer, including the main ICs.

  • Devices and Ports describes the I/O ports and the built-in I/O devices.

  • RAM Expansion describes the expansion features of interest to developers. It includes development guides for the RAM expansion modules.

  • Software describes the system software that comes with the computer, with emphasis on the ROM-in-RAM and Open Firmware features.

  • Abbreviations lists the abbreviations used in the developer note.
