Final Cut Pro/Final Cut Express
Final Cut Pro and Final Cut Express each provide a digital video-editing system that is scalable, nonlinear, and resolution-independent. Professional video editors can use these powerful tools to edit and finish broadcast-quality video in a variety of formats and resolutions. Software developers can extend the features of Final Cut Pro by creating effects with FXScript and by directly modifying project data using the Final Cut Pro XML Interchange Format.
