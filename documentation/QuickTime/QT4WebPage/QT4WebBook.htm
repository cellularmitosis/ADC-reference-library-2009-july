QuickTime Developer Series


Second Edition



ISBN 1558607803
850 pages		 This is the complete guide to creating QuickTime content and putting it on the web. It covers everything—from the right way to embed a movie in a Web page to the best techniques for combining scrolling text, Flash animation, MP3 audio, live streams, and virtual reality—in an engaging and easy to follow style.

Written for multimedia authors, Web heads, and anyone who wants to include sound or video on a website, this book provides clear, detailed, and often humorous guidance. This book contains a wealth of information you won't find anywhere else.

Includes a CD-ROM with QuickTime 5 Pro for Windows and Macintosh, as well as cross-platform tools from Apple, such as Plugin Helper and MakeRefMovie, and plenty of cut-and-paste examples of HTML and JavaScript.

This is the second edition of this award-winning book, completely revised and updated for QuickTime 5, with sections on new features such as media skins, XML, and cubic VR.
