Technical Q&A QTVR03
Using Digital Cameras

Q Why does Apple advise against using digital cameras? Did you try any of the ones that provide good numeric images? For a large production, the speed and relatively low cost of using digital cameras are big advantages compared to using conventional photography and PhotoCDs.

A Apple's only reason for not recommending digital cameras is image quality. If you find that the quality of the images you are getting with a digital camera are adequate, there is no reason that you shouldn't use them.

[Jun 01 1995]
